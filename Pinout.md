# Connector Pinout

## I2C/Wire
Pin | Function
----|---------
1   | GND (Ground)
2   | VCC (Power)
3   | SDA (Data)
4   | SCL (Clock)

## UART/Serial
Pin | Function
----|---------
1   | GND (Ground)
2   | VCC (Power)
3   | Rx  (Receive from host)
4   | Tx  (Transmit from host)

## SPI
Pin | Function
----|---------
1   | GND  (Ground)
2   | VCC  (Power)
3   | MISO (Master In)
4   | MOSI (Master Out)
5   | SCK  (Clock)
6   | CS   (Chip Select)
